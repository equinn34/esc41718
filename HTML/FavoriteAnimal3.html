    About Mickey Mouse

    Mickey Mouse will turn 90 years old on November 12th of this year, and there's not a gray hair on him. Sure, he's a little rounder, a little squatter, and he's been wearing the same clothes for decades, but all in all he still looks pretty good. Sure, Mickey hasn't had a movie in a long time, but his cheerful face remains one of the most recognizable images in the world, even beating out Santa Claus.
